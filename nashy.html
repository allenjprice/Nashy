Nashy Chord charts in any key, quick and easy.

How to use Nashy

  • Lyrics and chords go in the editor on the left.
  • Put your chords in [brackets], and place them in-line within your lyrics, like this: 
    [C]You and I and [Em7]George 
went [F]strolling through the park one [C]day
  • Choose your original key and your destination key from the menu
  • Click the "Nash it!" button to make your musician-ready chord chart
  • Copy and paste that chart into your favorite text editor
